# Project notes

## Core hypothesis

BCL6 perturbation in post-LVAD cardiomyocytes may shift cells toward or away from the recovery-associated transcriptional state.

## Important caution

The current ChIP target list gives BCL6 binding targets, but not whether BCL6 activates or represses each target in this exact cell context. Therefore, the first-pass perturbation model infers target direction from correlation with BCL6 expression. This is a heuristic.

## Stronger future version

Use CellOracle or another GRN method to infer cell-state-specific regulatory edges and simulate TF perturbation vectors.
